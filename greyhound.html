Greyhound Rescue

Greyhound Rescue is a not for profit charity that rehomes ex-racing greyhounds. Their current website is outdated and lacks cohesion. The mockup home page below incorporates a selective use of colour and gradients, as well as a logical information hierarchy to avoid cluttering the page with text whilst still providing necessary information and links.

Finding necessary links and information is made easy with the use of relevant iconography at the top of the web page. The footer of the web page displays all possible links on the page, with options to subscribe to the mailing list, donate, and follow Greyhound Rescue on social media.

 The literal greyhound motif is consistent throughout the web page, however more subtle motifs are also used, such as the bone shape. Contrast between imperfect, organic shapes and geometric shapes break up the page to create visual interest whilst maintaining order and cohesiveness.
