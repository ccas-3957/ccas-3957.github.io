Greyhound Rescue

Greyhound Rescue is a not for profit charity that rehomes ex-racing greyhounds. Their current website is outdated and lacks cohesion, making it harder to use, and by default, deterring potential donators. It is integral that the website and branding are updated to make the platform easy to use. 

This mockup website acts as a way of modernising the current brand by using consistent shapes, colours and motifs throughout. A logical information hierarchy as well as the selective use of colour avoids cluttering the page with text whilst still providing necessary information and links.

 The literal greyhound motif is consistent throughout the web page, however more subtle motifs are also used, such as the bone shape. Contrast between imperfect, organic shapes and geometric shapes break up the page to create visual interest whilst maintaining order and cohesiveness.

Finding necessary links and information is made simple with the use of relevant iconography at the top of the web page. The footer of the web page provides options to subscribe to the mailing list, donate, and follow Greyhound Rescue on social media.
